About

I've covered prisons in Washington state, corporate personhood in Delaware, and poultry workers in Maryland. After a brief detour to brush up on the latest in computational journalism at the University of Maryland, I now cover immigration and labor for Wisconsin Watch, where I also serve as half of the newsroom's data team.

I grew up in the Northwest, and I'd like to make it back one of these days — ideally to continue tinkering with data projects, though I could be just as happy as an old-fashioned beat reporter.
